Problem

Design

People love going to events, but ultimately have to encounter certain logistics as part of their event-going experiences. Venues have rules about what you can and can’t bring inside; and if you don’t know the area, parking can be a difficult task. Simply reaching the venue at all can be time-consuming and finding a place to eat before can be a pain. We sought to find a way to solve these issues for users while also sourcing the necessary information from user contributions. ChatterHub provides a way for users to ask questions and find answers about their end-to-end event-going experiences simply, quickly, and most importantly, reliably.
