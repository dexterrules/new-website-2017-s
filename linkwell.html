Design

Fall down tracking and alert products are regarded as assistive technology products. Assistive Technology (AT) is defined as devices ranging from low-tech and high-tech whichhelp individuals with disabilities, aging problems or rehabilitative difficulties after surgery. However, its use, particularly among elderly population, is often associated with social stigma, thus resulting in reluctant use of thetarget group.
