The Challenge

At eBay's annual intern conference held in summer of 2017, we were given a hack challenge. The challenge was to propose something that StubHub/eBay marketplace can integrate into their existing business model.

The Design: StubHub Local

We came up with a platform that connects artists and venues, thereby creating a local marketplace for audiences to go to events which are local, quick and pocket friendly. The platform has three different sides, a buyer side, which is a separate app along with artist and venue owner side which are bundled into a separate app.

The background

So, before Beck could see the listing, there was an entire transaction that happened between Jim Commons, the artist, and the venue owner. We call it, the venue marketplace.

Venue Marketplace

The venue marketplace basically is a different app which is meant just for artists and venue owners. Artists get to see a set of listings which were populated by venue owners. Artists can make a selection in the marketplace out of listed venues, and then send them a request.
