A Facebook chatbot leveraging Yelp's data to quickly recommend restaurant options, in a humanistic way.

My Role

I was responsible for design strategy and prototyping the product, from paper till Facebook bot.

The Problem

Food searching process is time-consuming. People have to go through ratings and reviews on Yelp to make restaurant/food decisions when they are new to a place.Also, humans don't use numbers to give their feedback  of any restaurant experience in a real conversation.
