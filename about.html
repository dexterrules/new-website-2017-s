Who am I?

I am Sanchit, an architect turned metathinking product designer who designs for human interactivity while keeping an eye on strategic decision making and eating nachos.

Yeah, threw in some heavy words in there. I know what you are thinking- is this some BS? Well currently I am pursuing masters in Human Computer Interaction Design at Indiana University. In the past, I have interned at StubHub (eBay), cofounded a startup, worked as a street fundraiser and have designed structures in Germany. This past semester, I worked with a real client to develop a rollout strategy while thinking about my own design philosophy. Overall, I am a work in progress and looking for full time opportunities in 2018.
